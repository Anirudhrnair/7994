INTRODUCTION TO WEB DESIGN

Web design is the process of creating and organising the look and layout of websites imagine it as decorating a room but for the internet. It involves arranging elements like text images and buttons so that they look good and are easy for people to use good web design ensures that a website is visually appealing easy to navigate and works well on different devices like computers,tablets and smart phone. Designers use various tools and technique to make sure the website not only looks nice but also helps users find what they need quickly and easily. In short, web design is all about making website that are both attractive and functional.
INTRODUCTION TO THE INTERNET

The internet is a global network that connects millions of computer and devices allowing them communicate with each other and share information. Originally developed in the late 1960's as a research project for the U.S department of defense it began as ARPANET, which aimed to enable communication between military and academic institutions. over the decades, the internet evolved with the introduction of key technologies like TCP/IP and the world wide web making it more accessible to the public. today it is an integral part of daily life ,revolutionizing the way we communicate access information and conduct bussiness

ADVANTAGE OF THE INTERNET
  • Access to information: the internet provides instant access to a vast amount of information on virtually any topic from academic research to how to guides and news updates.
  • Communication: it facilitates various forms of communication such as email, instant messaging,video call and social media, allowing people to stay connected regardless of geographical distance.
  • Education nd Learning: online courses, educational resources and virtual libaries making learning more accessible and flexible for people of all ages.
  • Convenience: the internet enables online shopping, banking and other services making it easier to manage daily tasks and transaction from the confort of one's home.
